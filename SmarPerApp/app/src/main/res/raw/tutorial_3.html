The SmarPer logs view lets you a history of past decisions. To access this, press "Usage data" at the main menu.



Refresh switch: Pressing will enable automatic refresh. List will be updated with the newest decisions every 20 seconds.

Long-pressing a decision: Allows you to update the recorded decision to another value. This can be used, for example, if you made a mistake when you were prompted.

You can also use the "Filter" option, accessible from the menu on the logs view, to adjust which decisions are displayed.

There is a legend for the filter, accessible via the "Help" option in the same menu. This legend explains what the various icons in the logs mean.
