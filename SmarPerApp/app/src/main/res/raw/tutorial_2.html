You may make a mistake at the prompt, or change your mind. To remove a decision from the cache, press "Clear the cache" at the main menu.

You will see a list of all your installed applications.


Choose the app from the list for which you would like to clear the cached decisions.


If there were cached decisions for this app, you will see the number of cached decisions that were cleared.

You will now be prompted again the next time the app requests a private resource.
